About StorePilot™

StorePilot™ is an economic simulation tool designed to support the preliminary evaluation of food retail concepts, restaurant locations and operational scenarios.

The tool converts simple operational inputs — such as orders per day, average ticket and cost structure — into indicative economic metrics including revenue run-rate, EBITDA and break-even thresholds.

Purpose of the tool

StorePilot™ is intended to help entrepreneurs, operators and advisors explore and compare potential business scenarios before making operational or investment decisions.

The service is designed for informational and analytical purposes only. Outputs are indicative simulations based on user-provided assumptions.

Project nature

StorePilot™ is an independent digital project focused on decision-support tools for the food retail and hospitality sector.

The project may evolve over time and may integrate additional analytical tools, advisory services or related initiatives.
