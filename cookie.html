Cookie Policy

Last updated: 29 March 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how StorePilot™ uses cookies and similar technologies on the website storepilot.eu and the related application funnel.

1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on a user's device by a website or application. They can be first-party or third-party, and can be session-based or persistent.

2. Cookies currently used

Based on the current technical configuration of StorePilot™, the website uses technologies strictly necessary for functionality, security and service delivery and, only with the user's consent, analytics tools to measure landing page traffic in aggregated form.

  • Technical / necessary cookies: used to ensure proper page delivery, basic security, language/session behavior and platform stability.
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics 4 may be activated only with the user's consent to collect aggregated data on visits, page views, traffic sources and essential landing page interactions.
  • Marketing / profiling cookies: not currently activated on the website.

3. Third-party services and external links

StorePilot™ may use or link to third-party services (for example Streamlit Cloud, Cal.com / Cal.eu, MailerSend, Google Analytics 4 and other Google services). When a user accesses such third-party environments, those providers may apply their own cookies or tracking technologies under their own policies.

For this reason, users should also review third-party cookie/privacy documentation when interacting with external tools.

4. Legal basis

Necessary cookies are processed under legitimate interest and/or necessity for service provision. Non-necessary analytics cookies are activated only with the user's consent where required by applicable law.

5. How to manage cookies

Users can manage or update cookie preferences through the banner available on the website and, in addition, delete cookies through browser settings. Limiting necessary cookies may affect the functionality of the website or application.

6. Updates

This Cookie Policy may be updated if the technical setup changes (for example changes to analytics, advertising tags, or new third-party scripts).
