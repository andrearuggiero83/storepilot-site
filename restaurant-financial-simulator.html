Restaurant financial simulator

StorePilot™ is a financial simulator designed for restaurant and food retail projects. It converts orders, average ticket and cost structure into revenue, EBITDA and break-even in just a few minutes.

What you can simulate

You can simulate revenue run-rate, operating margin, break-even and overall location sustainability. This makes it easier to compare scenarios using a coherent 4-wall logic.

Who it is for

It is useful for founders, operators, consultants and developers who want to validate or stress-test an economic model before making decisions.
